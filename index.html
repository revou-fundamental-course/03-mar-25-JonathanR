📖 About SMAN 1 Susukan

SMAN 1 Susukan is a public high school committed to producing intelligent, ethical, and patriotic graduates with strong IT skills and high integrity. We provide a balanced education that fosters academic excellence, character building, and technological competence.

🌟 Vision

“TERWUJUDNYA LULUSAN YANG CERDAS, BERINTEGRITAS, PATRIOTIK, MENGUASAI 'IT', DAN BERAKHLAK MULIA.”

🎯 Mission

  • 📌 Strengthening religious and moral education.
  • 📌 Promoting environmental awareness and sustainability.
  • 📌 Encouraging entrepreneurship among students.
  • 📌 Enhancing IT-based learning and infrastructure.
  • 📌 Instilling patriotism through national events.
  • 📌 Organizing science competitions and Olympiads.
  • 📌 Supporting sports, arts, and cultural activities.
  • 📌 Promoting literacy and critical thinking skills.
  • 📌 Building partnerships with external institutions.
